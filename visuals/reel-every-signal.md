# Reel — Every Signal

> Concept: A montage of unguarded human travel moments — each made possible by connection.
> Pipeline: /brief → /concept B → /script → /art-direction → /storyboard
> Status: Ready for generation

---

## Brief

```
Topic: eSIM for travelers — stay connected without friction
Angle: The freedom of a traveler who never thinks about connectivity —
       he's just fully present in the experience
Audience: Young adventurous men, 20-35, who prioritize experiences
          over logistics
Platform: Instagram Reels
Format: 9:16 vertical, 35 seconds
Tone: Bold, optimistic, companion-like — warm but sophisticated
Key message: Connection isn't the signal on your phone. It's what
             happens when you stop worrying about it.
Constraints: No product in frame. No phones visible on screen.
             Brand visual language (Connecting Line) must appear once
             as emotional pivot at 0:22.
```

---

## Script

```
BLOCK 1 — HOOK
Every traveler has a version of this moment — the one that makes
the whole trip worth it. And every single one of them happened
because they were connected.

BLOCK 2 — PRE-CTA
Stay to the end — there's one waiting for you too.

BLOCK 3 — WALKTHROUGH
First, the laugh you didn't see coming — on a rooftop, in a city
you couldn't pronounce two weeks ago. Then the call home at golden
hour, when someone asks "how is it?" and you don't have words.
Finally, the view you almost missed — because you looked up instead
of at a map, and somehow still found your way.

BLOCK 4 — TRANSITION
Connection isn't the signal on your phone. It's what happens when
you stop worrying about it.

BLOCK 5 — CTA
Comment ESIM to get yours before your next trip.
```

## Art Direction

```
Palette:
  #0D0B1E — Near-black indigo (opening shots, dark staging)
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (scene backgrounds, shadow zones)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (mid-scene color staging)
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (skin rim light, horizon glow)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (warm cloud tones, transition gradient)
  #F5E264 — Sunrise Yellow (Connecting Line accent, warmth peak)

Mood: A highlight reel of what it feels like to be fully present —
      not documenting a trip, living it.

Lighting: Golden hour throughout — warm directional rim light from
          behind or camera-left on every subject. No flat daylight.
          No artificial light except city glow in night shots.
          Shadows stay deep purple-grey (#2D2535). Every scene has
          a single dominant warm focal point against a cool backdrop.

Composition: Intimate and candid. Medium shots and close-ups dominant
             — faces, hands, reactions. No wide establishing shots
             except the final scene. Subjects always off-centre,
             rule of thirds. Camera feels handheld and present,
             not observational. 9:16 vertical throughout.

Environment: 4 distinct locations, each with its own light:
             1. Rooftop at dusk — warm city below, purple sky above
             2. Coastal cliff — golden hour, ocean horizon
             3. Mountain ridge — above clouds, lavender sky
             4. City street at night — golden ambient glow, movement

Texture: Cinematic grain throughout (ISO 800-1600 equivalent).
         Slight warmth grade on skin tones. No heavy LUTs.
         Matte finish — not glossy or oversaturated.

Typography: Bold Rethink Sans, white on dark. Max 4 words per screen.
            Yellow (#F5E264) for emphasis word only. Text always
            left-aligned, never centered.

Connecting Line: Appears once at 0:22 — the emotional pivot (Block 4).
                 Gradient: flamingo pink → golden orange → sunrise yellow.
                 Sweeps across screen left-to-right, large, bleeds
                 both edges. Duration: 1.5s. Acts as visual punctuation
                 — the moment the reel shifts from scenes to meaning.

References:
  1. Airbnb "Made Possible by Hosts" — human moments as full story
  2. Chris Burkard — golden rim light on subjects at altitude
  3. Patagonia brand films — candid movement, real skin, no retouching

Do NOT:
  - Use drone or aerial shots — keep the camera at human eye level
  - Show phones, apps, or any product
  - Use pop music or trending audio as primary feel guide
  - Cross-fade or dissolve — hard cuts only
  - Shoot in flat daylight — every frame needs the golden hour grade
  - Center the Connecting Line or make it small
```
