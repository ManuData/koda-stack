# ART DIRECTION — Instagram Ad: First Light Runner (Concept B)
> Skill: `/concept` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed

---

## Concept Brief

A single editorial hero image for a young, healthy traveller Instagram ad. No text. No logo. No CTA. No graphic overlays. Pure photography. A lone young traveller running along a coastal road, clifftop path, or ancient city street at the exact moment the sun breaks the horizon. They are the only person awake in this world. Silhouetted against a blinding golden sunrise, moving toward the light. The world entirely theirs.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (upper sky)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (mid-sky transition)
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (sunrise horizon, ground warmth, long shadows)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (sky mid-transition, warm haze)
  #F5E264 — Sunrise Yellow (sun orb, horizon brightest point)
  #C5AAFF — Lavender Mist (upper sky blend)

Mood: The private, earned feeling of moving through a beautiful place
      while it still belongs only to you. Solitude as freedom, motion as joy.

Lighting:
  Sunrise — sun at or just above the horizon, positioned ahead of the runner
  at the vanishing point of the path or road.
  Blinding warm orb of golden-yellow light (#F5E264, #FFB067) dead ahead.
  Sky gradient bottom-to-top: brilliant gold/amber at horizon → flamingo pink (#FFBFC0)
  → deep nebula purple (#7B2FD8) → deep indigo (#211F54) at top of frame.
  Ground catches warm amber light — road, path, or stone surface glowing orange-gold.
  Long morning shadows extend behind the runner, stretching toward camera.
  The figure is semi-silhouetted against the sunrise — warm rim light on edges.
  No fill light. No artificial light. Pure sunrise atmosphere.

Composition:
  3:4 portrait (1080×1440).
  Runner positioned mid-frame to lower-centre, seen from behind or slight three-quarter angle.
  Figure mid-stride — one foot off the ground, natural running posture, not posed.
  The path or road stretches ahead into the sunrise at the vanishing point.
  Sun orb sits at or near the vanishing point — the runner is moving directly toward it.
  Figure occupies roughly 25-35% of frame height — landscape and sky dominate.
  Long shadows extend behind the runner toward the camera in the lower frame.
  No other people, no vehicles, no crowds. Completely empty world.
  Negative space in sky is vast — upper two-thirds are atmospheric colour.

Environment:
  Choose ONE of:
  A) Coastal clifftop path — sea visible far below to one side, open sky ahead
  B) Ancient coastal or hillside road — stone surface, Mediterranean or similar
  C) Empty mountain trail at altitude — valley far below, open horizon
  No urban streets. No traffic. No signage. Pure natural or ancient landscape.
  Path or road surface catches and reflects the warm sunrise light.
  Vegetation minimal — grasses, low scrub, or bare stone. Nothing that blocks the horizon.

Texture:
  Ground surface texture warm and lit — golden stone, compacted earth, or ancient road.
  Sky smooth and atmospheric — no heavy cloud texture. Gradients clean and rich.
  Runner: natural athletic wear, nothing branded or logo-heavy. Functional, not fashion.
  Ultra clean editorial quality. No film grain. No heavy processing.

NO TEXT, NO GRAPHICS:
  No headline. No CTA. No logo. No caption. No watermark. No graphic overlay of any kind.
  Pure editorial photography only.

References:
  1. Chariots of Fire — light, motion, landscape — iconic runner-toward-horizon energy
  2. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-01-mountain-sunrise.png (warm amber-to-purple gradient, silhouette depth)
  3. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-02-beach-sunset.png (maximum warm-cool contrast, near-black shadows)

Do NOT:
  - Any text, type, headline, logo, watermark, caption or graphic overlay
  - Fitness model posing, flexing, or performance aesthetics
  - Protein shake, gym, or sports brand aesthetic
  - Other people, crowds, vehicles, or signs of civilisation
  - Flat or even lighting — sunrise drama is essential
  - Daytime or midday light — must be golden hour sunrise
  - Film grain, heavy processing, or oversaturated neon
  - Runner facing camera or in portrait orientation
  - Urban streets with signage, traffic lights, modern infrastructure
  - Branded sportswear with visible logos
```
