# ART DIRECTION — Instagram Ad: The Pause at the Overlook (Concept B)
> Skill: `/concept` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed

---

## Concept Brief

A single editorial hero image for a fashion-forward connected traveller Instagram ad. No text. No logo. No CTA. No graphic overlays. Pure photography. A stylishly dressed young traveller standing at a dramatic coastal or architectural viewpoint, completely absorbed by the view — phone loosely at their side, screen dark, forgotten. The phone is present the way a key would be — part of the person, not the focus. The world has won their full attention.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (upper sky)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (mid-sky transition)
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (side light on subject, golden hour warmth)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (sky transition, warm ambient)
  #F5E264 — Sunrise Yellow (brightest horizon point)
  #C5AAFF — Lavender Mist (upper sky blend)

Mood: The rare luxury of arriving somewhere so beautiful your phone goes quiet
      in your hand without you noticing. Stillness as the ultimate sophistication.

Lighting:
  Golden hour — sun low from the side, warm amber light (#FFB067) raking across
  the subject's profile, shoulder, and fabric from camera left or right.
  The light catches the texture of the linen clothing, creating soft warm shadows.
  Background view: softly lit by the same golden hour — sea, city, or valley glowing warm.
  Sky behind subject: flamingo pink (#FFBFC0) at the horizon fading through lavender
  (#C5AAFF) to deep nebula purple (#7B2FD8) and deep indigo (#211F54) at the top.
  No artificial light. No flash. Pure golden hour atmosphere.

Composition:
  3:4 portrait (1080×1440).
  Subject positioned lower-centre to slightly off-centre, seen from behind or at 45-degree angle.
  Face turned toward the view — profile or three-quarter, expression soft and absorbed.
  Phone held loosely at their side, arm relaxed, screen facing outward but dark/dim.
  The phone is visible but the last thing the eye finds — naturally at hip or thigh level.
  Background: vast coastline, city valley, or caldera view softly out of focus behind.
  Subject occupies roughly 40-50% of frame — sky and view dominate upper frame.
  Shallow depth of field — subject tack-sharp, background atmospheric and soft.
  A railing, stone wall, or terrace edge can be partially visible at the lower frame edge.

Environment:
  Dramatic viewpoint — one of:
  A) Amalfi Coast cliffside terrace — sea and colourful village below
  B) Lisbon miradouro — terracotta rooftiles and Tagus river stretching to horizon
  C) Santorini caldera overlook — volcanic cliffs, deep blue sea, white architecture
  The viewpoint feels private — not a crowded tourist spot.
  No other tourists, no barriers, no tour group signage.
  A stone wall, iron railing, or terrace parapet partially visible as foreground anchor.

Subject — Clothing & Style:
  Young woman or man, 25–35, quietly confident presence.
  Clothing: oversized structured linen blazer in warm sand or ivory,
  wide-leg trousers in the same warm neutral palette,
  simple leather sandals or mules.
  Minimal accessories — small leather bag or nothing. Simple jewellery if any.
  No visible clothing brand logos.
  Hair natural — wind-touched, not salon-perfect.
  Phone: modern smartphone, dark/neutral colour, held naturally at side, screen dark.
  The phone reads as personal object, not product.

Texture:
  Linen fabric texture warm and softly lit — the golden side light brings out the weave.
  Stone or iron of the viewpoint railing/wall: weathered, warm, textured.
  Background: atmospheric softness — sea haze, warm air, horizon glow.
  Ultra clean luxury editorial quality. No film grain. No heavy processing.

NO TEXT, NO GRAPHICS:
  No headline. No CTA. No logo. No caption. No watermark. No graphic overlay of any kind.
  Pure editorial photography only.

References:
  1. Peter Lindbergh — coastal fashion portraiture, human stillness against vast landscapes
  2. Shot on iPhone human series — natural device-in-hand, not a product shot
  3. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-03-seascape-dusk.png (full brand palette in one frame)

Do NOT:
  - Any text, type, headline, logo, watermark, caption or graphic overlay
  - Phone screen displaying visible content, apps, or bright glow
  - Phone raised toward camera or held up prominently — must hang naturally at side
  - Catalogue pose — static, hands on hips, facing camera directly
  - Tourist crowds, barriers, signage, or busy viewpoint infrastructure
  - Sportswear, casualwear, or visible brand logos on clothing
  - Flat or even lighting — golden hour side light is essential
  - Film grain, heavy processing, oversaturated
  - Subject facing camera directly — profile or three-quarter only
  - Graphic overlay, brand mark, connecting line
```
