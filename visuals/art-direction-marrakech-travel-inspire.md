# ART DIRECTION — Marrakech: Travel Inspiration Series
> Skill: `/image-ad-generator` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed + Reel
> Goal: Inspire people to travel to Marrakech — pure sensory and emotional pull
> Key message: Some places don't need a caption. They need a ticket.

---

## Concept

Seven shots. One woman. One medina. The complete arc of what it feels like
to arrive somewhere that rewires you. Not a tourism poster. Not a highlight reel.
The real thing — the sensory overload, the accidental discovery, the moment
you stop navigating and start belonging.

Each prompt is built around a single travel emotion. The image serves that
emotion — not the other way around.

---

## Shot 1 — The Overhead
> Emotion: **Overwhelm that becomes wonder**
> "You didn't expect this much beauty in one place."

```
GENERATION PROMPT
---
cinematic bird's eye overhead shot looking straight down onto a narrow
Marrakech medina souk alley, a solo young woman in a flowing white
wide-leg linen outfit with an orange leather crossbody bag is centered
in the cobblestone corridor, her body forming a diagonal as she reaches
toward a spice display, she appears small and joyfully lost against the
dark terracotta cobblestone floor, surrounding her on all sides:
circular wooden barrels overflowing with saffron yellow turmeric,
paprika red, cumin brown, dried herbs green, craft items in cobalt blue

calligraphy stroke in white surrounded by the full palette of Morocco,
warm amber market light from below catches the tops of the barrels,
the composition reads as graphic art before it reads as travel
photography, Kodak Portra 400 35mm film grain, warm rich color palette,
authentic medina atmosphere, no text no logos
```

