# ART DIRECTION — Instagram Ad: The Temple Keeper (Concept C)
> Skill: `/concept` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed

---

## Concept Brief

A single editorial hero image for a fashion-forward exotic traveller Instagram ad. No text. No logo. No CTA. No graphic overlays. Pure photography. A stylishly dressed young traveller inside an ancient jungle temple ruin — Angkor Wat corridors, a moss-covered Balinese shrine, or jungle-consumed stone architecture. Shafts of golden light through the jungle canopy. The subject looks like they belong to the place — not a tourist, not a model on location. History and the body in dialogue.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (light shafts through canopy, warm stone, skin warmth)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (warm ambient light, shadow transition)
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (deep jungle shadow, upper canopy gaps)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (deep shadow areas in stone corridors)
  Subject clothing: deep terracotta, warm ochre, burnt sienna, or structured natural linen

Mood: The specific feeling of touching something thousands of years old
      and understanding, suddenly, exactly where you are in time.
      Presence and place in equal measure — history and the body in dialogue.

Lighting:
  Shafts of golden-amber light (#FFB067) filtering through dense jungle canopy above,
  creating dramatic pools of warm light on ancient stone and the subject's skin and clothing.
  Dappled light patterns from leaves and canopy gaps on stone surfaces.
  Deep rich shadows in unlit areas — dark green jungle shadow, deep indigo-purple in stone corridors.
  Subject partially in a light shaft — warm amber on one side, deep shadow on the other.
  Background jungle: layers of deep green fading to near-black in shadow.
  No artificial light. No flash. Pure jungle canopy atmospheric light.

Composition:
  3:4 portrait (1080×1440).
  Subject positioned centre to slightly off-centre, occupying roughly 40-50% of frame height.
  Subject mid-movement or in a considered still pose — hand resting on carved ancient stone,
  walking through a stone corridor, or standing at the base of carved temple steps.
  Not posed for camera — engaged with the architecture, not performing for the lens.
  Ancient carved stone fills much of the frame — moss-covered reliefs, root-split walls, stone steps.
  Background: deep jungle green, stone, and shadow fading into atmospheric depth.
  Shallow depth of field — subject sharp, jungle and stone softly atmospheric behind.
  Upper frame: where canopy gaps allow, deep indigo-purple sky visible through the green.

Environment:
  Ancient jungle temple ruins — one of:
  A) Angkor Wat outer stone corridors and carved bas-relief walls, Cambodia
  B) Moss-covered Balinese stone shrine with frangipani and tropical roots
  C) Mayan or jungle-consumed stone architecture with enormous tree roots splitting walls
  Stone is ancient, weathered, moss-covered and root-split — not restored or clean.
  Jungle encroaches on all sides — enormous tree roots, vines, tropical foliage.
  The architecture feels discovered, not maintained.
  No tourist signage, no barriers, no modern infrastructure visible.

Subject — Clothing & Style:
  Young woman or man, 25–35, editorial presence — inhabits the space, not performing.
  Clothing: rich earth tones that harmonise with the golden stone and jungle warmth.
  Deep terracotta linen wide trousers with a structured ochre or warm ivory top, OR
  a flowing burnt sienna or rust-coloured dress with natural drape, OR
  structured natural linen in warm sand with one deep terracotta accessory.
  Natural fabrics only — linen, cotton, light silk. Nothing synthetic or sporty.
  Minimal accessories — simple jewellery, a leather or woven bag.
  No visible clothing brand logos.
  Hair natural — not salon-styled.
  Expression: contemplative, engaged, completely at ease in the ancient space.

Texture:
  Ancient stone texture is critical — carved reliefs, moss, weathering, root intrusion.
  Fabric: natural linen drape, warm and textured in the golden light.
  Jungle: layered green depth, organic and alive.
  Ultra clean luxury editorial quality. No film grain. No heavy processing.
  Skin tones warm and natural in the filtered jungle light.

NO TEXT, NO GRAPHICS:
  No headline. No CTA. No logo. No caption. No watermark. No graphic overlay of any kind.
  Pure editorial photography only.

References:
  1. Mario Testino — exotic location fashion, presence and place in equal measure
  2. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-01-mountain-sunrise.png (warm amber-to-purple, silhouette depth)
  3. Apocalypto (Mel Gibson) — jungle temple light and architecture reference for atmosphere

Do NOT:
  - Any text, type, headline, logo, watermark, caption or graphic overlay
  - Tourist behaviour — camera in hand, guidebook, posed selfie stance
  - Restored or clean temple — must feel ancient, moss-covered, genuinely wild
  - Visible modern infrastructure — barriers, signs, pathways, tourist facilities
  - Sportswear, casualwear, or visible brand logos on clothing
  - Flat or even lighting — shaft lighting through canopy is essential
  - Film grain, heavy processing, oversaturated
  - Subject blurred — must be tack-sharp
  - Overly dramatic horror or dark mood — warm, cinematic, not foreboding
  - Graphic overlay, brand mark, connecting line
```
