# ART DIRECTION — Terrace Dinner Trio
> Skill: `/image-ad-generator` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed
> Brief: Three friends (2 women, 1 man), rooftop terrace dinner, sport-chic style, barista coffee warmth

---

## Concept Brief

Three friends at a rooftop terrace table — mid-conversation, leaning in, sharing something on a phone.
Not a dinner party. Not a photoshoot. A moment that started as coffee and became a night nobody planned.
The table is the anchor. The city is the stage. The connection is the product.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  Candlelight / tungsten  — amber warm: #FFB067
  Dusk sky upper frame    — lavender mist fading: #C5AAFF → #DDB3E8
  Deep shadow / background— near-black brand indigo: #211F54
  Skin ambient glow       — flamingo warm: #FFBFC0
  Outfit base layer       — ecru / cream: #EDE8DC
  Table surface           — warm linen: #F5F4FF

Mood:
  The dinner that started as coffee and became the night you didn't plan.
  Three people, one phone, zero performance. The city watches — they don't.

Lighting:
  Dual source — warm and intimate:
    1. Overhead pendant lamps (rattan or exposed Edison bulb): amber tungsten
       glow falling from above-behind onto shoulders, nape of neck, table surface.
       Warm pool of light on faces — soft, not harsh.
    2. Last golden hour rim light from low sun behind the group, slightly
       camera-right: creates soft warm halo on hair and outer shoulders.
    3. Single taper candle on table: small warm flicker adding depth to lower frame.
  Upper background: transitions from warm amber near rooftop to cool lavender-dusk
  sky — the brand's temperature split (warm lower / cool upper).
  Never: overhead fluorescent, cold white, flat even lighting, midday sun.

Composition:
  Format: 3:4 vertical (1080×1350)
  Camera: elevated 45° angle — above eye level, angled down slightly onto the group.
    Not a top-down flat lay. Not eye level. Between the two.
  Table: anchors the bottom third — espresso cups, glasses, plates visible
    as supporting elements, not the hero.
  Subjects: fill the mid-frame as a connected unit.
    Woman 1 (left) — leaning slightly toward the man, looking at the phone he holds.
    Man (center) — holding phone at slight angle so all three can see, soft laugh.
    Woman 2 (right) — leaning in from the other side, hand on the table or touching
    her espresso cup, head tilted toward the group.
  Background: city skyline or rooftop garden in the upper third — mid-to-full bokeh.
    Warm bokeh orbs from distant building lights and pendant lamps behind.
  Depth layers: subjects sharp → railing/plants mid-bokeh → skyline bokeh → dusk sky.
  Never: perfectly symmetrical framing, direct camera stare, wide full-body shot,
    text or graphic overlays inside the image.

Outfits (sport-chic — no logos):
  Woman 1:
    High-waist dark slim trousers (charcoal or deep navy)
    Fitted ribbed knit top — warm ecru or ivory
    Blazer draped over the back of her chair — camel or sand tone
    Layered thin gold chains (2–3 layers)
    Hair: natural waves or loose low bun
  Woman 2:
    Midi length satin slip dress — dusty mauve #C4A0A0 or sage #8FAF8F
    Fitted white long-sleeve underneath
    Clean white or bone sneakers partially visible
    Single delicate gold chain
    Hair: down with natural texture, or half-up
  Man:
    Tailored tapered jogger trousers — dark olive or charcoal
    Clean fitted white tee
    Unstructured overshirt jacket open — sand or ecru tone
    Clean white minimalist sneakers
    Single fine gold chain at neckline
    Hair: natural, relaxed

Table details (barista/coffee vibes):
  2–3 small espresso cups (one with crema visible)
  1 latte art glass — flat white or cortado, steam rising softly
  Wine glasses (partially filled, warm amber liquid)
  Shared plates — small dishes, Mediterranean or tapas style
  1 taper candle — slim, ivory, small warm flame
  Linen napkins, no branded items, no menus visible
  Table surface: warm stone or aged wood — natural texture

Expression:
  Candid, unguarded, mid-laugh or leaning in with soft attention.
  Not posing. Not performing. The camera caught them, not the other way around.
  One person is speaking or reacting to something on the phone.
  The other two are in a moment of genuine response — soft laugh, raised eyebrow,
  or leaning closer. No forced smiles, no stiff poses, no stock-photo energy.

Texture:
  35mm film photography, Kodak Portra 400, natural warm grain.
  Slightly desaturated midtones — candlelight and gold chains stay saturated.
  Realistic skin texture — no airbrushing, no plastic skin, no smooth filters.
  Never: HDR, oversaturated, Instagram-filter warmth, heavy preset look.

Do NOT:
  - Text, typography, or graphic elements inside the image
  - Any subject looking directly into camera
  - Overly formal dinner setting (no white tablecloth fine dining)
  - Matching outfits on any two people
  - Logos on any garment or item
  - Bright overhead lighting or cold white light sources
  - Wide full-body shot showing feet and full table
  - Forced smiles or performative expressions
  - Empty or sterile rooftop — environment must feel lived-in and warm
---
```
