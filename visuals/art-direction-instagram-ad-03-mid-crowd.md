# ART DIRECTION — Instagram Ad: Mid-Crowd, Eyes Closed (Concept B)
> Skill: `/concept` | Format: 4:5 | Platform: Instagram Feed

---

## Concept Brief

A single editorial hero image for an Instagram ad. The subject is a young traveller (20s) in the middle of a dense, colourful festival crowd — laughing, eyes closed or looking skyward, arms loose and free. The crowd behind them is motion-blurred warmth. The subject is tack-sharp. The Avelsky Connecting Line sweeps across the mid-ground as a graphic companion, large and in motion.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (upper sky, background anchor)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (upper sky transition)
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (crowd lights, background blur)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (Connecting Line gradient start, crowd warmth)
  #F5E264 — Sunrise Yellow (Connecting Line gradient end, typography accent)
  #C5AAFF — Lavender Mist (upper sky blend)

Mood: Abandon. The kind of joy you can only feel when you've stopped trying to hold onto it.
      The most radical act — letting go in the middle of chaos.

Lighting:
  Golden hour festival energy — warm golden orange and flamingo pink dominating lower and mid frame.
  Crowd lights and lanterns blurring into warm bokeh behind subject.
  Upper frame transitions to deep nebula purple (#7B2FD8) and lavender (#C5AAFF).
  Single light source from slightly above, warming subject's face and shoulders.
  No studio. No flash. Practical festival/ambient light only.

Composition:
  4:5 portrait (1080×1350).
  Subject centred or slightly left, occupying mid-to-upper frame.
  Subject tack-sharp — face, expression, arms. Everything else motion-blurred warmth.
  Connecting Line graphic enters from right edge, arcs loosely across mid-ground near subject,
  without touching them — feels like a companion moving alongside.
  Shallow depth of field — extreme bokeh on crowd background.
  Headline text lower third, white, structural.

Environment:
  Dense festival crowd, night market, or outdoor carnival at golden hour / dusk.
  Blurred lanterns, string lights, or market stall colours in background.
  Warm amber-gold and flamingo pink fills the background blur.
  No identifiable logos, no props. Pure energy and atmosphere.

Texture:
  Clean digital editorial — no film grain. Commercial photography quality.
  Skin tones natural and warm. Subject not over-processed.
  Connecting Line has subtle luminosity/glow in the warm mid-ground.

Typography:
  Rethink Sans Bold.
  Primary headline: "Every moment. No limits." — white, lower third.
  Accent: "limits" in #F5E264 (Sunrise Yellow).
  CTA: "Connect" — small white text, bottom of frame.
  Placement: lower third, clean and readable against dark or blurred background.

Connecting Line (graphic overlay):
  Yellow (#F5E264) on warm bright background — solid yellow version.
  Form: loose, fluid open loop — not a closed shape, not a border, not an underline.
  Scale: fills roughly 50-60% of frame width.
  Position: sweeps in from right edge, arcs loosely near subject in mid-ground.
  Applied on top of photography layer. Feels like it is travelling with the subject.

References:
  1. VISUAL_DNA.md — branding-05-connecting-line-buyer-persona-fun.png (candid, joyful unguarded energy)
  2. VISUAL_DNA.md — branding-01-connecting-line-buyer-persona-sea-summer.png (yellow line on warm/bright scene)
  3. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-02-beach-sunset.png (maximum warm-cool contrast)

Do NOT:
  - Posed, stock-photo-style expressions or body language
  - Flat or even lighting anywhere in the frame
  - Sharp background — must be bokeh/motion blur
  - Connecting Line used as a border, underline, or divider
  - Small or centred text
  - Generic travel clichés (passport, airplane, luggage)
  - Oversaturated neon without gradation
```
