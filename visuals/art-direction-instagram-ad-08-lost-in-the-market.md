# ART DIRECTION — Instagram Ad: Lost in the Market (Concept C)
> Skill: `/concept` | Format: 3:4 | Platform: Instagram Feed

---

## Concept Brief

A single editorial hero image for a fashion-forward traveller Instagram ad. No text. No logo. No CTA. No graphic overlays. Pure photography. A young, stylishly dressed traveller immersed in a vivid, colourful market — a Moroccan souk, Southeast Asian night market, or Turkish bazaar. The subject is tack-sharp and calm inside a sensory explosion of colour and life around them. Style as quiet confidence against beautiful chaos.

---

## Art Direction

```
ART DIRECTION
---
Palette:
  #FFB067 — Golden Orange (market light shafts, spice colours, warm ambient)
  #FFBFC0 — Flamingo Pink (fabric stalls, flower markets, warm background elements)
  #211F54 — Deep Space Indigo (deep market shadows, canopy gaps)
  #7B2FD8 — Avelsky Purple (deep background shadow areas)
  Subject clothing: warm white, sand, terracotta — calm against the vivid market

Mood: The confidence of moving through somewhere entirely unfamiliar
      and feeling completely at home in your own skin.
      The world is loud; they are quiet inside it.

Lighting:
  Golden hour light cutting through market canopy, awnings, or stall overhangs.
  Shafts of warm amber light (#FFB067) falling through gaps in the canopy above,
  creating pools of warmth on the subject and market floor.
  Background market elements: vivid but slightly softer — natural ambient market light.
  Subject lit by a warm directional shaft — one side warm amber, shadows falling naturally.
  Depth of field: subject tack-sharp, background market colour explosion beautifully blurred.
  No flash. No artificial studio light. Pure market atmosphere.

Composition:
  3:4 portrait (1080×1440).
  Subject positioned centre to slightly off-centre, occupying roughly 40-50% of frame height.
  Subject mid-movement — reaching toward a market display, turning mid-conversation,
  or walking through a narrow stall corridor. Not posed, not static.
  Background: explosion of out-of-focus colour — reds, saffron, cobalt, turquoise, deep purple.
  Shallow depth of field — subject razor sharp, the market world behind blurs into
  a rich abstract of colour and light.
  Market elements partially frame the subject — hanging fabrics, stall edges, spice displays.
  The contrast between the calm, considered subject and the vivid chaos is the composition.

Environment:
  Choose ONE of:
  A) Moroccan souk — hanging lanterns, spice pyramids, rich textiles in deep reds and golds
  B) Southeast Asian dusk market — paper lanterns, tropical flowers, warm food stall light
  C) Turkish bazaar — hanging fabrics, ceramic displays, cobalt and terracotta tones
  Market feels authentic and alive — not a set, not a tourist attraction recreation.
  Narrow corridor or stall-lined path creates natural depth.
  Canopy or overhead covering creates the shaft lighting effect.

Subject — Clothing & Style:
  Young woman or man, 25–35, naturally styled — not a model pose, not a catalogue shot.
  Clothing: deliberately calm and considered against the market chaos.
  Warm white or sand linen — wide trousers, a draped top, or an oversized shirt.
  One terracotta, warm amber, or neutral accessory — a bag, a scarf, simple jewellery.
  Natural fabrics only — linen, cotton, light silk. Nothing synthetic-looking.
  Footwear: leather sandals or simple mules.
  No visible brand logos on clothing.
  Expression and body language: engaged, curious, unhurried — completely at ease.

Texture:
  Rich texture throughout — fabric, spice, ceramic, woven material in background.
  Subject clothing: clean linen texture, natural drape.
  Ultra clean editorial quality. No film grain. No heavy processing.
  Skin tones warm and natural in the market light.

NO TEXT, NO GRAPHICS:
  No headline. No CTA. No logo. No caption. No watermark. No graphic overlay of any kind.
  Pure editorial photography only.

References:
  1. Mous Lamrabat — fashion subjects inside vivid cultural environments
  2. VISUAL_DNA.md — branding-03-connecting-line-buyer-persona-vibes.png (mood and colour staging)
  3. VISUAL_DNA.md — color-02-beach-sunset.png (maximum warm-cool contrast)

Do NOT:
  - Any text, type, headline, logo, watermark, caption or graphic overlay
  - Posed catalogue stance — static, hands on hips, facing camera directly
  - Tourist behaviour — map in hand, camera around neck, guidebook
  - Visible clothing brand logos
  - Flat or even lighting — shaft lighting through canopy is essential
  - Overly clean or styled market — should feel real and alive
  - Film grain, heavy processing, oversaturated neon
  - Subject blurred or out of focus — they must be tack-sharp
  - Empty or quiet market — the background should feel full of life and colour
```
